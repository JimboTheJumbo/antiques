About Us
Our Story & Mission

A Family Tradition

J. Austin Antiques is a family owned store in Amherst, MA focused entirely on the American Arts and Crafts movement. We carry Mission furniture, pottery, lighting, original art, and metal work, all hand-selected.

We have an ever-changing selection of Mission furniture, art, pottery, lighting, metal work and more.

The Arts and Crafts movement pushed back against mass production in the late 1800s. The work is honest: quartersawn oak, visible joinery, hand-thrown clay, hammered metal. Nothing hidden, nothing fake.

Placeholder Image

We pick every piece ourselves. Whether you have been collecting for years or just found the style, you are welcome to come look around.

We are at 31 South Pleasant Street in Amherst and at the Green Market on Saturdays. Stop in: something new comes in all the time.
